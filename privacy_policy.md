## English Version

### Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how MindCircle ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our iOS application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

### Information We Collect

#### 1. User-Generated Content
- **Chat Messages**: Your conversations with AI experts are stored locally on your device using UserDefaults
- **Group Configurations**: Group names, topics, and member settings you create
- **Chat History**: All messages and timestamps within your groups

#### 2. Data Sent to Third-Party Services
- **AI Service Provider**: Your chat messages are sent to SiliconFlow (https://siliconflow.cn) via our Cloudflare Worker proxy to generate AI responses
- **API Endpoint**: https://siliconflow-api-proxy.henrywu573.workers.dev
- **Model Used**: DeepSeek-V3 AI model

### How We Use Your Information

#### App Functionality
- Store your chat history locally on your device
- Send your messages to AI service providers to generate expert responses
- Maintain group configurations and settings

#### What We Do NOT Do
- We do NOT collect personally identifiable information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
- We do NOT upload your data to our own servers
- We do NOT share your data with third parties for marketing purposes
- We do NOT track your usage across other apps or websites
- We do NOT use your data for advertising

### Data Storage and Security

#### Local Storage
- All chat history and group data are stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults
- Data persists until you delete the app or manually clear app data
- We do not have access to your locally stored data

#### Data Transmission
- Messages are transmitted to AI service providers via HTTPS encryption
- We use a Cloudflare Worker as a secure proxy to protect API credentials
- Your messages are only used to generate AI responses and are not stored by us

### Third-Party Services

#### SiliconFlow API
- **Purpose**: Generate AI expert responses
- **Data Shared**: Your chat messages and conversation context
- **Privacy Policy**: https://siliconflow.cn/privacy (please review their policy)
- **Data Retention**: Governed by SiliconFlow's data retention policies

#### Cloudflare Workers
- **Purpose**: Secure API proxy to protect credentials
- **Data Shared**: Your chat messages in transit
- **Privacy Policy**: https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/

### Your Rights and Choices

#### Data Access
- You can view all your data within the app at any time
- All data is stored locally and accessible only to you

#### Data Deletion
- Delete individual messages or entire groups within the app
- Uninstall the app to permanently delete all local data
- Contact us to request information about data sent to third-party services

#### Data Portability
- Your data is stored in standard formats on your device
- You can export or backup your data through iOS backup mechanisms

### Children's Privacy

MindCircle is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

### Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
- Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and on our support page
- Providing in-app notifications for significant changes

### Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

- **Email**: support@mindcircle.app
- **GitHub**: https://github.com/Henrywu573/chatbox
- **Support Page**: https://henrywu573.github.io/public_doc/

### Legal Compliance

This app complies with:
- Apple App Store Review Guidelines
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) principles
- California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) principles
- Other applicable data protection laws
