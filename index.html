â“ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is MindCircle?
A: MindCircle is an AI group chat app that allows you to converse with multiple AI experts, each with different personalities and professional backgrounds. You can create topic-based groups, invite suitable experts to join discussions, and gain multi-perspective insights and advice.
Q: How do I create a new group?
A: Tap the menu button in the top-left corner, select "Create New Group," and enter the group name and discussion topic. The system will automatically recommend suitable AI experts based on the topic, or you can customize your expert team.
Q: What is YOLO mode?
A: YOLO (You Only Live Once) mode is an auto-discussion feature. Type "YOLO" in the chat to activate it, and AI experts will automatically engage in deep discussions. You can observe different perspectives colliding. Type "stop" to end the auto-discussion.
Q: How do I @mention a specific expert?
A: Type @expert-name or @expert-title in your message. The mentioned expert will prioritize responding to your question. For example: "@FoodCritic Any good restaurant recommendations nearby?"
Q: Is my data safe?
A: Your chat history is stored locally on your device (UserDefaults) and is not uploaded to our servers. Message content is sent to the AI service provider (SiliconFlow) to generate responses but is not used for other purposes. We do not collect any personally identifiable information.
Q: Does the app require an internet connection?
A: Yes, MindCircle requires an internet connection to chat with AI experts. Your message history is saved locally and can be viewed even when offline.
Q: How do I manage group members?
A: In the group chat interface, tap the member avatars in the top-right corner to view, add, or remove group members. You can flexibly adjust your expert team based on discussion needs.
Q: Is the app free?
A: Yes, MindCircle is currently completely free to use, with no in-app purchases or subscriptions.
